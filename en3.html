Salted fish is a project with a distinctive blockchain spirit. It has similarities with the rise of bitcoin and doggy coin. It is fully started in the community, and attracts more and more business leaders to become enthusiastic volunteers to participate in the community. Following this great experiment of decentralized spontaneous community construction, salted fish is creating a perfect and thorough decentralization and unique community culture in mdex, Hufu, tea, biter and coin an, which is more friendly and more suitable for the global blockchain user group

Salted fish adopts auxiliary mining. The salted fish auxiliary mining program is a mining program based on NVIDIA GPU. It is compatible with windows and Linux, and supports mining 58 virtual coins including bitcoin and SF. As a platform, all the machines with computing ability can participate in mining. If the reward is obtained, the calculation force of the mine pool is distributed according to the calculation force level of the machine. After the mining assistant is started, the system will copy the previous mining operation, realize the fully automatic mining mode, so that users can really free their hands and make money
